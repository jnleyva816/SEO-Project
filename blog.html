Rev2Rotors: Flying into a new Frontier

Rev2Rotors is a company with multiple sites, but the main headquarters operates in Lakeland, Florida. We offer a variety of services related to one growing industry: kits to convert cars into helicopters.

The car-to-helicopter conversion kits vary in how much they convert a car, let alone how each brand of car requires a different kind of kit. This is why proper registration on the website allows for Rev2Rotors to tailor a kit that meets the specifications of every user.

The CEOs of Rev2Rotors originate in Florida, a state where demand for helicopter conversion kits has been increasing rapidly over the years, thanks in no part due to increased road construction and the general nature of traffic in the Sunshine State.
